Oil Change

Your engine depends on oil to function. It helps keep engine parts cool by lowering friction, reducing wear, providing lubrication, forming a seal between the pistons, rings, and cylinder walls, and providing lubrication.

Check Engine

Your car's engine is its heart. It is a sophisticated device designed to transform the heat from burning gas into the force that rotates the vehicle's wheels. K.A.MECHANIC SERVICES has the knowledge to interpret check engine light codes, take care of any necessary maintenance or repairs.

Transmission

Leaks, aging, or contamination are all potential causes of low transmission fluid. It can result in dryness and particle buildup, which can cause component grinding, overheating, and transmission failure.

Tires

As recommended by the manufacturer of your vehicle, K.A.MECHANIC SERVICES technicians will examine, rotate, and balance your tires. When it comes time to change them, we'll figure out what size and type of tire you need for the vehicle and make sure they're mounted correctly.
